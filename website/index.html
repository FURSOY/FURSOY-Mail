Purpose

A faster way to use Gmail on Windows.

FURSOY Mail is a desktop Gmail client. With your permission, it reads and synchronizes Gmail messages so it can detect verification codes locally, show new-mail notifications, and let you copy codes without searching through your inbox.

Gmail access also enables the mail actions you choose, including sending messages, marking messages as read, and moving messages to trash. Mail data is processed on your device and is not sent to or stored on FURSOY servers.
